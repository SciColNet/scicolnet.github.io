Venue

This workshop will happen at Leiden University, one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in Europe, located in the historic city of Leiden, Netherlands. Leiden University is known for its world-class research and vibrant academic atmosphere. Leiden offers a perfect setting for our conference.

Address

Gorlaeus Building - Leiden University
Einsteinweg 55, 2333 CC Leiden, Netherlands

Getting Here

  • By Train : Leiden is easily accessible by train, with direct connections to Amsterdam, Rotterdam, and The Hague. The venue is a short walk or bike ride from Leiden Central Station.
  • By Air : Schiphol Airport (Amsterdam) is just 20 minutes away by train, offering easy access for international travellers.
  • By Car : Parking is available near the venue.

Explore Leiden

Leiden is a picturesque city filled with canals, historic landmarks, and a rich cultural heritage. Take a stroll through its cobbled streets, visit museums like the National Museum of Antiquities, or enjoy a boat tour through the canals.
